About Me

Hi, I'm Jake, a freelance artist and designer from Perth, Western Australia.

For the past few years I have worked predominantly in animation, where I've been an animator, storyboard artist, and background artist for various animated series and projects.
As a graphic designer, I've freelanced for several years now, with my main area of focus being in logo and web design for small local businesses, and illustrated posters for functions and events.

Currently, I am teaching 2D animation at Curtin University, attending AWARD School, working on various web series, and doing freelance design work.
