About Me

Hi, I'm Jake, a freelance artist and designer from Perth, Western Australia.

For the past few years I have worked predominantly in animation, working for various online entertainment companies like Nerdist, CollegeHumor, and Explosm Entertainment as an animator, storyboard artist and background artist.
As a freelance graphic designer, some of my local clients have included Event Stadium, Notre Dame University, and Perth Eye Hospital.

Most recently, I completed AWARD School 2018 and have been teaching animation at Curtin University. When I'm not working, I like to write, read, draw, play piano, and I'm currently learning to speak Spanish.
